Bio

Christopher Ashby, a cyber security veteran has more than 25 years of proven experience participating in a broad range of corporate initiatives including architecting, implementing, and supporting information-security solutions in direct support of business objectives. He has held multiple positions including hands on engineering, advanced threat analytics and analysis, to executive leadership responsible for executing numerous global cyber programs.

In his most current role he focuses on reducing operational cost and business risk by actively identifying and preventing cyberattacks against people, process, and technology for a large biopharmaceutical oganization.
