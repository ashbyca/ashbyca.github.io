Bio

Christopher Ashby, a cyber security veteran has more than 25 years of proven experience participating in a broad range of corporate initiatives including architecting, implementing, and supporting information-security solutions in direct support of business objectives. He has held multiple positions including hands on enginerring, advanced analytics and analysis, to executive executing global programs.

In his most current role he focuses on reducing operational cost and business risk by actively identifying and preventing cyberattacks against people, process, and technology for a large biopharmaceutical oganization.

Over the last 10 years he has held various hands on strategic roles including serving as a Cyber Intel Watch Commander at Digitalware, where he provided strategic and tactical threat intelligence capabilities to New York Cyber Command (NYC3).

Prior to this he was a senior director of security at CBS Corporation where he assembled a best-in-class global security program that was aligned to the NIST Cybersecurity Framework. One of his many responsibilities during this time was creating a threat intelligence program that was integrated into every division within the organizations. These included but not limited to Privacy, M&A, Physical Security, Content Security, and Live Broadcast Events.
