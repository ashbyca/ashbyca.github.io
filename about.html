Bio

Christopher is a Senior Cyber Security Engineer, Global Security Solutions at Alexion. He is responsible for proactively identifying and responding to cyber threats that could impact the companies' mission for developing treatments for patients with severe and life-threatening diseases.  Mr. Ashby brings more than 20 years of cyber security experience across many business verticals and government institutions. 

Prior to joining Alexion Pharmacuticals, he served as Cyber Intel Watch Commander, at Digitalware Inc. Mr. Ashby hold various industry recognized security certifications including ISACA CISM, ISC(2) CISSP, CCSP, and has attended various executive development programs at Harvard University, Carnegie Mellon University, and Ithaca Collage, where he sits on the advisory board helping develop the next generation of cyber security processionals.
