Bio

Christopher is currently the Head of Cybersecurity at Fresenius Medical Care. He and his team is charged with the development and delivery of next-generation cyber security capabilities through influencing, collaborating, and providing the most innovative solutions across the organization. Mr. Ashby brings more than 20 years of cyber security experience across many business verticals and government institutions. 

Prior to joining Fresenius Medical Care, he was a Senior Security Lead at Alexion Pharmaceuticals. Mr. Ashby hold various industry recognized security certifications including ISACA CISM; CRISC; ISC(2) CISSP; CCSP; and has attended various executive development programs at Harvard University, Carnegie Mellon University, and Ithaca Collage, where he sits on the advisory board helping develop the next generation of cyber security professionals.
